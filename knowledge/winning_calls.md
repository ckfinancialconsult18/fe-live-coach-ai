# Winning Calls — Calls That Resulted in a Policy Written

## Overview

This file is a curated collection of calls where a policy was written. Study these for patterns in tonality, discovery, objection handling, and closing. Every winning call has something to teach.

> These calls represent the highest-scoring interactions in our library. When the AI coach references "top performer patterns," it is drawing from content in this file.

---

## What Makes a Winning Call

Analysis of top-performing calls consistently shows:

1. **Strong opening** — Agent states purpose clearly within 15 seconds; rapport is established before benefits are mentioned.
2. **Deep discovery** — Agent asks at least 3–5 open-ended questions before presenting any product.
3. **Emotional anchor** — Agent gets the prospect to verbalize who they are protecting and why it matters to them.
4. **Proactive objection handling** — Agent addresses price and waiting period *before* the prospect raises them.
5. **Assumptive or trial close** — Agent does not ask "do you want to do this?" — they ask a logistics question that presupposes yes.
6. **Silence after the close** — Agent asks the closing question and waits.
